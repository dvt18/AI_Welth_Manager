## Slide 2 — Brief about the idea

**Title:** Meet Arya — A Wealth Manager for Every IDBI Customer, Inside Their Mobile App

**Body (2 columns):**

*Left column — The idea, in one paragraph:*

> **Arya is an AI wealth advisor with a face, embedded inside the IDBI mobile banking app.** It watches every rupee a customer earns, spends, saves, borrows and invests — across IDBI, external brokers, PF, NPS, credit cards — and turns that view into **personalised, in-language, in-context advice**. Fourteen specialist AI agents sit behind a conversational avatar; every recommendation is explainable, one-tap actionable, and stays inside IDBI's cloud. It democratises HNI-grade wealth advice at the cost of an LLM inference call.

*Three lines that separate Arya from every wealth product in India today:*
- **Bank-embedded, not standalone** — no new app, no re-KYC, no separate login (HDFC SmartWealth is a *separate* app; Groww / INDmoney / Kuvera live outside the bank entirely).
- **Behavioural + investment AI in one brain** — nobody in India fuses spend patterns with holdings; peers do one or the other.
- **Proactive, not reactive** — Arya reaches out; every other app waits to be opened.

*Right column — Priya's Monday morning:*

> **7:42 AM** · Priya's salary of ₹1.2L hits her IDBI account.
>
> **7:43 AM** · Arya greets her on the IDBI home screen:
>
> *"Good morning Priya — three quick wins today:*
> *1️⃣ ₹6L is idle at 3% — sweeping ₹4L to an FD earns you **₹16,400/yr**.*
> *2️⃣ Your credit-card interest is 42% — I can switch you to 24% and save **₹14,000/yr**.*
> *3️⃣ Your 'Home 2032' jar is 3% behind — a ₹1,800 SIP top-up catches up.*
> *Say Yes — I'll do the rest."*
>
> **7:44 AM** · Priya taps **Yes to all three.** Zero forms. **₹30,000+ back in her pocket** this year.

*What Arya can see (with consent, via RBI AA):*
🏦 IDBI accounts · cards · loans · 📈 Groww / Zerodha SIPs · 💼 EPFO PF · PFRDA NPS · 💳 credit-card spends · 🎯 goals · salary pattern · life events

**Footer (large, bold, centred):** **"Like CRED for your wealth — but with an advisor sitting inside your bank, in your language, saving you real money every month."**

**Speaker notes:** Two upgrades vs the earlier version — (1) Priya now gets *three* Arya wins in a single moment, not one, so the depth of the platform is visible in 20 seconds; (2) the 3-line "what separates Arya" bullets are the elevator pitch — memorise them, every Q&A comes back to these three. Don't read the 14-agent count aloud on this slide — that lives on Slide 4. Keep the CRED line; it lands in 2 seconds with any Indian audience.

## Slide 3 — Opportunities (Differentiation, USP, Problem-Solving)

**Title:** Why Arya Wins — 3 Questions Every Judge Will Ask

**Body (3 blocks — one per question):**

### A. How is Arya different from every existing idea?

*Three cuts that separate Arya. No player in India today does all three — because doing all three requires the bank itself to build it.*

| Dimension | Where existing players stop | Where Arya starts |
|---|---|---|
| **Location of advice** | HDFC SmartWealth = separate app · Groww / Kuvera / Zerodha = outside the bank · Traditional RMs = phone / branch | **Inside the IDBI mobile app** — reuses SSO / mPIN / UPI; zero context-switch |
| **AI depth** | ICICI iWealth / SBI YONO Invest = static screens · INDmoney = aggregation only · robo-advisors = risk-score → allocation, no reasoning | **14 specialist AI agents** fusing spend + holdings + credit + goals + life events on one brain |
| **Direction of engagement** | Everyone waits for the customer to open the app | **Arya reaches out** — salary day, life events, market moves, goal drift |

> *One-line:* **No one has combined bank reach + AI depth + proactive engagement — because doing all three needs the bank itself to build it.**

### B. How will Arya solve the problem?

*The problem today — 5 hard breaks:*
1. Advice is **priced-out** — human RMs are HNI-only (₹50L+ AUM)
2. Advice is **spend-blind** — peers see holdings *or* transactions, never both
3. Advice is **reactive** — apps wait; the 80% who never open them get nothing
4. Advice is **channel-avoided** — PDFs, RM calls, branch visits are ignored by retail
5. Advice is **text-only, English-first** — 300M+ vernacular users left out

*Arya's answer — one mechanic per break:*

| The break | Arya's mechanic |
|---|---|
| Priced-out | **14 AI agents** deliver HNI-grade advice at ~₹5–8/user/month LLM cost |
| Spend-blind | **Account Aggregator + IDBI Core** give Arya *every rupee* — IDBI + brokers + PF + NPS + cards |
| Reactive | **Proactive triggers** — salary-day check-in, life event, rate move, goal drift; every conversation is Arya-initiated |
| Channel-avoided | **Chat + avatar inside the IDBI app** the customer already opens 12×/month for UPI |
| Text-only | **7 regional languages at launch** (Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, Punjabi) — parity with HDFC SmartWealth's 9-language tier |

> *Bonus:* **Bill Negotiator** and **Credit Coach** put real money back every month — CC interest, insurance premiums, personal-loan rates, CIBIL boost. No Indian wealth app does this today.

### C. USP — the one-line + top 5

> **"Every IDBI customer gets a personal wealth manager — with a face, in their language, with access to every rupee they own — inside the app they already use."**

*Top 5 differentiators (full list of 16 in Approach.md §4):*

1. **Bank-embedded, not standalone** — inside the IDBI app; zero re-KYC, zero context-switch
2. **Behavioural + investment AI in one brain** — spend + holdings + credit + goals fused (nobody else fuses)
3. **Proactive advisor** — reaches out on salary day, life events, market moves
4. **Money-back-in-pocket layer** — Bill Negotiator (CC interest, insurance, loan rates) + Credit Coach (CIBIL) — unique in India
5. **Explainable with cited sources** — every "Why?" cites the RBI rate, SEBI rule, product-doc line (Perplexity-style)

Verbally walk the three sections: (A) *"No one does all three cuts because it needs a bank to build it — that's why we're at IDBI."* (B) *"Five breaks, five mechanics — Arya is engineered against each one."* (C) *"One line, top 5 — the rest lives in Approach.md §4."* HDFC SmartWealth is the incumbent to call out by name in (A); every other competitor is a shallower cut.

## Slide 4 — List of Features Offered by the Solution

**Title:** What Arya Does For You

**Body (5 customer-verb buckets):**

**👀 Watches your money — all of it**
- Tracks every rupee across IDBI + Groww + Zerodha + PF + NPS + credit cards
- Categorises every spend automatically (like CRED does for cards — but everywhere)
- Flags idle cash earning nothing

**💬 Talks to you like a real advisor**
- Chats in your language — English, Hindi + 7 regional (Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, Punjabi)
- Explains **why** before recommending anything — with cited sources
- Remembers you across sessions ("last year you were nervous about SIP timing — you gained 18%")

**🤔 Answers the money question of the moment**
- *"Can I buy an iPhone?"* — checks your budget, goals, purpose
- *"Am I saving enough tax?"* — 80C, NPS, old vs new regime
- *"Should I sell this stock?"* — profit, tax impact, alternatives
- *"How do I boost my CIBIL score?"* — top drags + 3 micro-actions (Credit Coach)

**💰 Puts money back in your pocket** *(new — Bill Negotiator agent)*
- Cuts your credit-card interest — from 42% p.a. down to 24% via IDBI's product catalog
- Re-shops your health/term insurance at renewal — same cover, ₹8K cheaper
- Optimises your personal-loan / EMI rates when better options exist
- Cancels the 3 unused subscriptions bleeding ₹1,400/mo (Subscription Audit)

**⚡ Acts in one tap**
- Start SIP · Book FD · Switch fund · Set a goal · Auto round-up to Liquid Fund
- Every action goes through **your bank** — no new app, no re-KYC, no separate login

**Footer (small text):** *Behind the scenes: **14 specialist AI agents** (Spend Analyzer, Income Intelligence, Risk Profiler, Goal Planner, Portfolio Doctor, Retirement Planner, Purchase Advisor, Stock Advisor, Tax Optimizer, Product Recommender, **Bill Negotiator**, **Credit Coach**, Nudge Engine, Compliance Guardrail) — all running inside IDBI's cloud. Full agent architecture in Slide 15 appendix.*

**Live example — Priya's Monday morning (5 steps in 45 seconds):**

| # | Step | What actually happens |
|---|---|---|
| 1️⃣ | **Trigger** | 7:42 AM — salary of ₹1.2L credited to Priya's IDBI a/c |
| 2️⃣ | **Perceive** | ₹6L idle in savings · CC utilisation 62% (interest 42% p.a.) · "Home 2032" jar 3% behind pace |
| 3️⃣ | **Reason** | 4 of 14 agents fire in parallel — **Nudge Engine**, **Bill Negotiator**, **Goal Planner**, **Compliance Guardrail** |
| 4️⃣ | **Advise** | *"Priya, 3 wins today — sweep ₹4L (₹16,400/yr), switch CC to 24% (₹14,000/yr), top-up SIP ₹1,800/mo. Say Yes."* Each with a **"Why?" citation** (RBI rate, IDBI rate table, jar-math) |
| 5️⃣ | **Act** | Priya taps **Yes to all 3** → sweep-FD booked · CC balance transferred · SIP mandate signed. **All in 45 sec.** |

**Trust callouts along the flow:**
- 🔒 **Every fetch consented** via RBI AA — nothing pulled silently
- 📝 **Every reco cited** — RBI rate / SEBI rule / product-doc line
- 👋 **Non-discretionary** — Arya suggests, customer approves; guardrail auto-appends disclaimers

**Speaker notes:** Two upgrades vs the earlier 4-step version — (1) added an explicit **Trigger** step so proactivity is visible before "Perceive"; (2) added **agent fan-out** inside Reason (only relevant agents of 14 fire — fast + cheap).
